# TrustShield
TrustShield is a sophisticated phishing detection system designed to help users navigate the digital world safely. In an era of increasing cyber threats, our platform serves as a first line of defense against cyberfraud.
The core mission of TrustShield is to provide real-time protection by analyzing text patterns and identifying high-risk triggers. We aim to bridge the gap between complex cybersecurity protocols and everyday user needs, making safety accessible to everyone.
When you paste a message or URL, our backend engine performs a dual-layer analysis:
  Dataset Matching: Scans for known phishing trigger words and phrases.
  AI Evaluation: Uses machine learning to recognize patterns common in scam communications.
  Risk Grading: Assigns a risk level (Low, Medium, or High) with transparent reasoning.
